1. Shed siding repair

1.1. Tasks to perform

  • Remove the large section of rotten plywood and replace it with a fresh piece of T1-11 siding.
  • Patch medium-sized holes (e.g., the small round woodpecker hole in the top corner) with plywood patches.
  • Coin-sized holes and smaller will be caulked with silicone to seal them off.

1.2. Materials

  • One sheet of T1-11 siding: $45
